Who We Are

Redefining Affordable Travel
in Southeast Asia

CheapestGo is a US-based online travel agency (OTA) platform built specifically for the Southeast Asian traveler. We leverage real-time hotel pricing, curated deal curation, and a seamless mobile-first experience to consistently surface prices that major platforms overlook — passing those savings directly to our users.

📍 Headquartered in New York, USA · Built and operated by a Philippines-based development team · Serving travelers across Southeast Asia with a 12% transparent markup — meaningfully cheaper than the industry standard of 15–25%.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make travel affordable and accessible for every Southeast Asian traveler by delivering transparent pricing, unbeatable deals, and a seamless booking experience.

Our Vision

To become Southeast Asia's most trusted value-first travel platform — where travelers always know they are getting the best price without sacrificing quality or convenience.

🎯 The Problem We Solve

  • Major OTAs built for Western markets charge 15–25% markups
  • Hidden fees and currency conversion surprises
  • Poor local payment support (GrabPay, GCash, Maya)
  • Deals not curated for SEA destinations
  • Slow, data-heavy apps on low-bandwidth connections

✅ Our Solution

  • Transparent 12% flat markup — no hidden fees
  • Real-time pricing via our partners (Duffel, Mystifly, TravelgateX, ONDA, Rakuten)
  • GrabPay, local cards & Stripe payment support
  • Flash deals & packages curated for SEA travelers
  • Sub-1.5s load times optimized for mobile SEA networks

Market Opportunity

A $47B Market Waiting
for a Better Option

Southeast Asia's online travel market is one of the fastest-growing in the world, driven by a rising middle class, increasing smartphone penetration, and a culture that values travel. Yet the dominant platforms were not built with SEA travelers in mind.

📈 Market Size & Growth

  • $47B+ projected SEA online travel by 2025 (Google–Temasek)
  • 12–15% annual growth rate in SEA digital travel
  • 85%+ of SEA bookings are made on mobile devices
  • 400M+ internet users across 6 core SEA markets
  • Post-pandemic travel surge still accelerating across the region

🎯 Target Customer Profile

  • Age 22–45, mobile-first, value-conscious SEA traveler
  • Core markets: Philippines, Thailand, Indonesia, Malaysia, Vietnam, Singapore
  • Books 1–4 leisure trips per year
  • Average booking value: $80–$150 USD
  • Price is the #1 booking decision factor (72% of travelers)

Business Model & Revenue Projections
Business Model

Markup-Based Revenue with
Transparent Pricing

CheapestGo operates on a straightforward markup model — travel rates are sourced at wholesale via our partners (Duffel, Mystifly, TravelgateX, ONDA, Rakuten), a 12% markup is applied at checkout, and customers always see the final all-in price. No hidden fees. No last-minute surprises.

💰 Revenue per Booking ($100 avg)

  • Gross markup (12%): $12.00
  • Stripe international fee (−5.4%): −$5.40
  • Partner API costs (−2%): −$2.00
  • Net margin per booking: ~$4.20 (4.2%)

📊 Breakeven Analysis

  • Monthly infrastructure cost: ~$500
  • Net per booking at $90 avg: ~$3.78
  • Bookings needed to break even: ~133/month
  • That's just 4–5 bookings per day
  • Realistic by Month 4–5 with moderate marketing
